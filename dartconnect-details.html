Project Description

When I started working at DTC I knew I would be interested in any opportunities that may be out of my comfort zone. However, graphic design was not something I ever expected to contribute to our development team, especially since I have a tendency to hold myself to an almost unattainably high standard in these scenarios. Luckily our software development team was awesome to work with. As a beginner in UI/UX design, I had the opportunity to collaborate, refine ideas, and iterate alongside experienced teammates. Through that months of feedback and discussion we landed on the final iteration that we are not only using today but one I am proud of. Hopefully our end product satisfied the mission of improving the look, feel, accessibility, and usability of the app because I believe it did.
